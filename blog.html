CD REVIEW: Scott Reeves Jazz Orchestra – Without A Trace

Wednesday, June 06, 2018

New York Big Band leader and composer Scott Reeves is someone whose music I wish I'd discovered sooner. His new release, Without A Trace, contains originals and standards, colourfully orchestrated and recorded with an intimate, club-like ambience.

CD REVIEW: Frank Harrison and Brigitte Beraha - The Way Home

Wednesday, June 06, 2018

Notions of love and home are given shape in this recording by the lyrical piano of UK’s Frank Harrison, and the ethereal vocals of London-based Brigitte Beraha.Her words to the opening track The Man Who Cycled from India for Love tell the wonderfully romantic story of the Indian artist who cycled to Sweden in the '70s to create a new home with the woman he loved.On his journey he lived by drawing, but a painting of the woman was no substitute: "I couldn’t paint you at all/ You knew/ I’d find my way back/ Straight to you."
